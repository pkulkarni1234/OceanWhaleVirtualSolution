    Ocean Whale Virtual

    As Ocean Whale Virtual Pvt. Limited was founded by team of experienced Mariners. Ocean Whale Virtual Private Limited is a pioneer in Shipping Business located at India & Dubai . We help in opening a window for opportunities for vessel owners /Managers across the word for all their requirements. We are an ISO 9001:2015 Company registered in Pune, the Financial Capital and Shipping Hub of India, and from our office in Pune offer our reliable and cost effective services to our Clients all over the world. We also hold a Recruitment and Placement Services Licence (RPSL) and MLC 2006 Certificate issued by the Director, Seamens Employment Office, Pune. ISO :

    Being an ISO 9001:2015 certified company means that the quality of services we provide are of internationally acceptable standards. Being RPSL and MLC 2006 Certified Company provides the necessary assurance to our Clients as well as to our seafarers that our recruitment process is fair and as per guidelines of MLC 2006. As DOC holder we can manage full vessel in House. We are proud to be owners of a Crew Boat named ‘MB BSA’ which has been recently acquired and is being deployed for transportation of offshore seafarers and we are sure that this is just the beginning with plans for rapid and planned expansion. Purchase and/or more vessels of different types are being considered for being added to our fleet.

    Vision

    Our Vision is to create a niche for ourselves in this challenging and dynamic industry and be considered the business partners of choice by Shipowners / Shipmanagers looking for reliable mangers to manage their assets.



    Mission

    As DOC holder our aim is to tender our quality service of managing the vessel with our team of experienced Mariners. Our Mission is to provide maximum number of jobs to Indian Seafarers thereby increasing the employment share of Indian Seafarers in the world market, to provide cost effective and reliable services to our clients.
